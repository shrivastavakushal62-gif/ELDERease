📱 Learn Mobile

Easy step-by-step guides – just for you!

💬 How to Use WhatsApp

WhatsApp helps you send messages and make free calls to family!

1
Open WhatsApp

Look for the green icon with a white phone on your mobile. Tap on it to open.

💡 Tip: The icon looks like this → 📱
2
Find a Contact

You will see a list of your contacts. Find the name of the person you want to message.

💡 Tip: Tap on the magnifying glass 🔍 to search by name
3
Type a Message

Tap the box at the bottom of the screen. Type your message using the keyboard.

💡 Tip: You can also press the 🎤 microphone to send a voice message!
4
Send the Message

Press the green arrow button ➡️ on the right to send. Your message is sent!

5
Make a Video Call

Open a chat, then tap the video camera icon 📹 at the top right corner to start a video call!

💡 Tip: Make sure you are connected to WiFi for free calls!

💳 How to Make Online Payments

You can pay bills and shop online using UPI – it is safe and easy!

1
Open a UPI App

Look for apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm on your mobile. Tap to open.

💡 Common apps: Google Pay 📱, PhonePe 💜, Paytm 🔵
2
Choose "Send Money" or "Pay"

Look for a button that says "Send Money", "Pay", or "Transfer". Tap on it.

3
Enter Phone Number or Scan QR

Type the phone number of the person you want to pay. OR scan the QR code shown at the shop.

💡 QR code looks like a square black-and-white pattern 📷
4
Enter the Amount

Type how much money you want to send. Double-check the amount before sending!

⚠️ Always check the amount carefully before sending!
5
Enter Your UPI PIN

Enter your secret 4 or 6 digit PIN. Your money will be sent immediately!

🔒 Never share your PIN with anyone – not even family!
⚠️ Stay Safe! Never share your OTP or PIN with anyone on phone. Banks never ask for PIN.

📞 How to Make a Phone Call

Making a phone call is very simple. Follow these steps!

1
Open the Phone App

Look for the green phone icon 📞 on your home screen. Tap on it.

2
Go to Contacts

Tap on "Contacts" at the bottom of the screen to see your saved contacts list.

💡 Your contacts are people whose numbers you have saved
3
Find the Person's Name

Scroll through the list or type the name in the search box at the top.

4
Tap Their Name and Call

Tap the person's name, then tap the green phone button 📞 to call them.

5
End the Call

When you are done talking, press the red button 🔴 to end the call.

📷 How to Use the Camera

Take photos of your family and special moments easily!

1
Open the Camera App

Look for the camera icon 📷 on your home screen. Tap on it to open.

2
Point at What You Want to Photograph

Hold your phone and point the back of it towards the person or object you want to take a photo of.

💡 Hold your phone steady with both hands for a clear photo!
3
Press the Round Button

There is a big round white button at the bottom. Press it once to take the photo!

4
View Your Photo

Tap the small square in the corner to see your photo. It is saved in your Gallery!

5
Share on WhatsApp

Open the photo, tap Share, choose WhatsApp, and send to your family!

💡 Sharing memories with family is wonderful!
