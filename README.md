# 🧓 ELDERease — Smart Elderly Care Assistant

*Designing accessible and supportive technology for elderly care, safety, and independent living.*

---

# 🌍 Overview

ELDERease is a human-centered elderly care support platform designed to help senior citizens manage daily healthcare routines, emergency situations, medication schedules, and digital accessibility through a simple and elderly-friendly interface.

As aging populations continue to grow globally—especially in countries like Japan—the need for accessible and supportive healthcare technology is becoming increasingly important.

Many elderly individuals face challenges such as:
- forgetting medications
- difficulty using smartphones
- delayed emergency response
- lack of digital accessibility
- dependency on caregivers for simple tasks

ELDERease aims to reduce these challenges by creating a supportive digital environment that prioritizes:
- simplicity
- accessibility
- safety
- independence
- emotional comfort

The project combines healthcare assistance, emergency support, and guided digital accessibility into one integrated system.

---

# 🎯 Core Objective

The primary goal of ELDERease is to improve the quality of life and independence of elderly individuals through accessible and supportive digital technology.

The platform is designed to help users:
- manage healthcare routines
- access emergency assistance quickly
- improve digital confidence
- stay connected with caregivers
- maintain safer daily habits

Unlike complex applications that may overwhelm elderly users, ELDERease focuses on:
- simplicity
- clarity
- large accessible interfaces
- user-friendly navigation
- minimal interaction complexity
